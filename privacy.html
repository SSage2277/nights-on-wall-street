1) Data We Collect

  • Account data: username, password hash, account identifiers.
  • Gameplay/account state: balance, claims, wins, and progression-related records.
  • Security/operations data: IP address, user agent, session data, trusted device metadata, and admin audit events.
  • Local browser storage used to improve session continuity and on-device UX.

2) How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and protect accounts.
  • Store and restore game progress.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized admin access.
  • Review claims and operate customer support and moderation workflows.

3) Cookies and Sessions

We use secure session cookies for login state and server-side access control. If cookies are disabled, some account features may not work.

4) Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with infrastructure providers only as required to host and operate the service.

5) Data Retention

We retain account and security records as long as needed for service operation, fraud prevention, and legal obligations.

6) Security

We use technical controls including hashed passwords, session controls, and rate limiting. No system is 100% secure; users should use strong unique passwords.

7) Your Choices

You can request account deletion/support actions through the operator. Some records may be retained where required for security or legal reasons.

8) Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will include a revised effective date.
